List of my 6 favourite Black Mirror episodes

In my opinion, in our days, one of the best tv-show on air is Black Mirror. This is a Netflix production, it has four complete seasons, and the fifth one's first episode is just come out nowadays.

Each season and each episode is a fully unique story. If you are new and have never heard of Black Mirror, however, you want to start watching, you can easily pick up any episode you want, you will understand everything. Every episode plays in a not so bright but a highly advanced technological future and every episodes has a strong message and criticism about mankind.

I would like to represent my top six "must-see" episodes from the first four seasons, so here is my list:
(Ordered by show date)

1. National Anthem

Season 1, Episode 1

Storyline: United Kingdom's Prime Minister has been blackmailed by a man, who kidnapped the popular princess of the Royal Family. The kidnapper demanded only one thing: the Prime Minister has to intercourse with a pig on live, otherwise the princess will die.

Reason why I choose: It teaches you, don't be a malicious news consumer and mind your own business.

2. Be right back

Season 2, Episode 1

Storyline: After Martha lost her boyfriend, named Ash, she can't handle her grieve. She decides to try a beta version A.I., which database contains all data about Ash and imitate him in a robot body, which is a realistic human body, basicly a copy of Ash.

Reason why I choose: It will show you "what's gone is gone", however, grieving can be really hard, if you don't let it (him) go, it's just get worse, longer and harder.

3. Nosedive

Season 3, Episode 1

Storyline: Imagine a world where you can rate literally everybody around you. Higher score means bigger reputation and fame therefore, everybody wants to be well rated. If you're ever get underrated because you're honest and not fake anymore, comeback is nearly impossible.

Reason why I choose: It's a possible critic to our social media society, for the people who wants to reach achievement and shows their fake, momentary happy moments for strangers but not themselves.

4. San Junipero

Season 3, Episode 4

Storyline: This is a world where you can upload your mind to the cloud when you are on the edge of your life and soon, death's gonna knock on your door.

Reason why I choose: Fun fact: I love the 80's so much, the atmosphere and the vibe is absolutely stunning! Also, this is the highest rated episode on IMDb, so no words needed!

5. Man against fire

Season 3, Episode 5

Storyline: This episode will guide us into a soldier's everydays. There's one main purpose: kill the enemy! The more you'll kill, the more reward you'll get. But what if you look behind the scene and realise you were deeply manipulated?

Reason why I choose: This is one of the lowest rated episode, however, this episode has got a really powerful message about how mans lying and manipulating everything and everybody to defeat their enemies and keep their power.

6. USS Callister

Season 4, Episode 1

Storyline: Nanette's first days at work seems perfect, except that her psychopathic boss will steal her DNA and keep her clone in captivity in his own world. Int his world he is the captain of the USS Callister and everybody in this world is terrorised and tortured.

Reason why I choose: Even if you're more like a Star Wars fan, than a trekkie, this one is for you. Even better if you liked The Collector by John Fowles, it's like a mash-up by these two.
